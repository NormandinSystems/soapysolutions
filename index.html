Ceramic Coating Protection

A semi-permanent layer of nano-ceramic quartz that bonds to your clear coat. It provides an extreme hydrophobic effect, unmatched gloss, and defense against Montreal's harsh elements.

  • Multi-year 9H hardness protection
  • Extreme water & dirt repellency
  • Protection against UV rays & oxidation
  • Deep, mirror-like candy gloss finish

Multi-Stage Paint Correction

Erase years of automatic car wash damage. Our meticulous compounding and polishing process safely removes defects from your clear coat to reveal a flawless finish.
